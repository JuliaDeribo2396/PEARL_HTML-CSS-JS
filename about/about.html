About us

About the pearl market

The Pearl Market recognized a need for more at home self-care by creating and hand-making products such as Scented Soy Candles, Plaster Candle Product, Plaster Home decorations, and so much more! We have created a lifestyle brand of products and pride ourselves in being eco-friendly. We use the best quality ingredients in our products while also being conscious of our environmental footprint by offering reusable, recyclable packaging. In addition, we have a jar return program at each store and offer candle refills. Our eco-friendly packaging combined with our self-care mission allows our community to bring the spa home and feel good about it.

Our mission

The Pearl Market is committed to creating scented products that enhance everyday living, fostering joy and well-being through sensory experiences.

Our values

Sustainability: Commitment to environmentally friendly practices and materials, including recyclable and reusable packaging and products free from dyes, gluten, phthalates, parabens, or animal byproducts. Quality: Dedication to producing high-quality, clean-burning candles and wellness products. Innovation: Continuous development of new products and scents to delight and inspire our customers. Community: Building strong relationships with customers and partners, and supporting our local community. Integrity: Conducting business with transparency, honesty, and respect for all stakeholders.
