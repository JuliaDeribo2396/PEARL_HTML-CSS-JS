Our story

The Pearl Market recognized a need for more at home self-care by creating and hand-making products such as Scented Soy Candles, Plaster Candle Product, Plaster Home decorations, and so much more! We have created a lifestyle brand of products and pride ourselves in being eco-friendly. We use the best quality ingredients in our products while also being conscious of our environmental footprint by offering reusable, recyclable packaging. In addition, we have a jar return program at each store and offer candle refills. Our eco-friendly packaging combined with our self-care mission allows our community to bring the spa home and feel good about it.
